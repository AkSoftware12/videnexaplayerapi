Modern Online Video Player

Fast, responsive video player with playlist, custom controls, quality selector and simple hosting support. Use the built-in demo videos or replace with your own URLs.

How it works

  1. Paste a direct MP4/MOV/WebM URL in the search box and click Play.
  2. Or click any video in the gallery to load into player.
  3. Use controls for seek, volume and fullscreen.
